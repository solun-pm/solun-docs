Solun is a service that allows you to share files, text and send emails with end-to-end encryption, ensuring your privacy is never compromised.

## What is the purpose of Solun?

Solun is designed to address the current privacy gaps, providing an independent, open service for sharing files, sending texts and emails with end-to-end encryption. With Solun, you no longer have to depend on external services like WhatsApp, Telegram, Signal and more for private, encrypted communication.
